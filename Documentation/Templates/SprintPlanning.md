# Sprint <n> planning

## Goal

The one-sentence outcome this sprint pursues.

## Dates and capacity

Start and end dates; capacity in points, accounting for known interruptions (plan to roughly 80% of raw capacity).

## Committed backlog

The tasks pulled in, each meeting the Definition of Ready, with estimate, priority, and assignee.

## Risks and dependencies

What could block the sprint and how it is handled.

## Completion checklist
- [ ] Goal stated.
- [ ] Every committed item meets the Definition of Ready.
- [ ] Capacity realistic with slack.
- [ ] Risks and dependencies noted.

## Review checklist
- [ ] Commitment matches capacity.
- [ ] Meets DocumentationStandards.
