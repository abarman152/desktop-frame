# <feature> PRD

## Problem

The user problem this solves, traced to a persona and a job from the Notion Product documentation. Cite the pain point.

## Goals and success metrics

What success looks like and how it is measured. Tie to Success Metrics.

## Non-goals

What this deliberately does not address.

## Users and jobs

The personas served and the jobs to be done this addresses.

## Requirements

The capabilities required, prioritized. Each is an outcome, not a UI detail.

## Acceptance criteria

Testable conditions that define done. QA verifies against these.

## Open questions

What is unresolved and who owns resolving it.

## References

Links to the Notion personas, jobs, pain points, and any research.

## Completion checklist
- [ ] Problem traced to a persona, job, and pain point.
- [ ] Success metrics defined.
- [ ] Requirements stated as outcomes.
- [ ] Testable acceptance criteria written.
- [ ] Non-goals and open questions listed.

## Review checklist
- [ ] Every requirement traces to a user need.
- [ ] Acceptance criteria are verifiable.
- [ ] Meets DocumentationStandards.
