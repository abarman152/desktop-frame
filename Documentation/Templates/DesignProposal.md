# Design proposal — <topic>

## Problem

What is being solved and why now. The constraints in play.

## Proposal

The proposed approach, in enough detail to evaluate.

## Alternatives

Other approaches considered, with their merits and costs. Include doing nothing.

## Trade-offs

What the proposal gives up to get what it gains.

## Impact

What it affects: architecture, performance, security, the plugin API, saved data.

## Open questions

What must be resolved before this can be decided.

## Completion checklist
- [ ] Problem and constraints stated.
- [ ] Proposal detailed enough to evaluate.
- [ ] Real alternatives compared, including doing nothing.
- [ ] Impact across architecture, performance, and security assessed.

## Review checklist
- [ ] If accepted, an ADR is written to record the decision.
- [ ] Meets DocumentationStandards.
